About us

Hello and welcome everyone! We are a quaint little candy shop located in the heart of Almont, just half an hour outside of Ottawa. Our doors first opened in 1976 and we’ve been a favourite to the locals ever since. All the employees are specially trained to handle our products as everything is made in house, offering customers a firsthand view at the products, excellent gifts as well. Come visit on a weekend drive and see what everyone’s talking about.
